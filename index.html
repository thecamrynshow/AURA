The Science

PNEUOMA experiences are built on decades of research in polyvagal theory, biofeedback therapy, and game-based learning. Every mechanic is designed to activate the parasympathetic nervous system.

  • Heart Rate Variability Training
  • Respiratory Sinus Arrhythmia
  • Game-Based Learning Research

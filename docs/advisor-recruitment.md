# Advisor Recruitment Materials

## Overview

PNEUOMA is seeking 3-4 advisors with expertise in clinical psychology, education, veteran services, and/or health technology. Advisors will provide strategic guidance, industry connections, and credibility as we seek investment and partnerships.

---

## Advisor Compensation

**Standard Offer:**
- 0.25% - 0.50% equity (vesting over 2 years)
- No cash compensation (pre-revenue startup)
- Time commitment: 2-4 hours/month

**What We Provide:**
- Regular updates on progress
- Opportunity to shape impactful technology
- Recognition on website and materials
- Network access to other advisors and investors

---

## Ideal Advisor Profiles

### 1. Clinical Advisor
**Title:** Clinical Psychology or Psychiatry Advisor

**Ideal Background:**
- Licensed psychologist, psychiatrist, or clinical social worker
- Expertise in trauma, anxiety, or nervous system regulation
- Experience with digital therapeutics or telehealth
- Academic credentials (PhD, MD) preferred
- Published research a plus

**What We Need From Them:**
- Clinical validation of approach
- Study design guidance
- Connections to research institutions
- Credibility for grant applications

**Where to Find:**
- University psychology departments
- Digital therapeutics companies (alumni)
- APA (American Psychological Association) members
- Polyvagal Institute faculty
- LinkedIn searches: "digital therapeutics" + "psychologist"

### 2. Education Advisor
**Title:** Education & SEL Advisor

**Ideal Background:**
- School counselor, principal, or superintendent
- SEL program implementation experience
- EdTech experience a plus
- District-level connections
- CASEL familiarity

**What We Need From Them:**
- School pilot connections
- Curriculum alignment guidance
- Pricing feedback for education market
- Credibility with districts

**Where to Find:**
- CASEL conferences and networks
- EdSurge community
- State counseling associations
- LinkedIn searches: "SEL director" or "school counselor"
- Local school district administrators

### 3. Veterans Advisor
**Title:** Veterans Mental Health Advisor

**Ideal Background:**
- VA clinician or administrator
- Veteran service organization leader
- PTSD treatment experience
- Understanding of VA procurement
- Veteran status a plus

**What We Need From Them:**
- VA pilot connections
- Understanding of veteran needs
- Navigation of VA bureaucracy
- Credibility with DoD/VA

**Where to Find:**
- VA Innovation Network (VHA Innovation)
- Veterans service organizations (VFW, American Legion)
- VA medical center staff
- LinkedIn searches: "VA mental health" or "veteran PTSD"
- Military transition organizations

### 4. Health Tech Advisor
**Title:** Digital Health Advisor

**Ideal Background:**
- Previous founder or executive in health tech
- Experience with FDA digital therapeutics
- Fundraising experience
- Strategic partnerships background

**What We Need From Them:**
- Strategic guidance
- Investor introductions
- Partnership strategies
- Regulatory navigation

**Where to Find:**
- Digital health accelerators (Rock Health, StartX)
- Previous digital therapeutics exits
- Health tech VC networks
- LinkedIn searches: "digital therapeutics founder"

Section 7

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does this take from the school day?

Most implementations use 3-12 minutes per session. Teachers report this time is recovered through smoother transitions and fewer disruptions.

Do teachers need extensive training?

No. The platform is designed to be intuitive. We provide a 45-minute training session, and teachers can start using it immediately.

What if students don't engage?

PNEUOMA uses game mechanics that meet students where they are. Early data shows 90%+ engagement rates. We also provide troubleshooting support if engagement dips.

Is this just another SEL program?

No. SEL focuses on social-emotional skills and awareness. PNEUOMA works at the physiological level ‚Äî training the nervous system itself. It complements SEL, it doesn't replace it.

What about students with special needs?

Many experiences are designed with sensory sensitivities in mind. We also have specific content for regulation challenges common in special education settings.

Can parents use this at home?

Yes. The PNEU app allows home practice, and we provide parent guides for continuity between school and home.

## Design Tips

1. **Dark backgrounds** throughout ‚Äî matches brand
2. **Accent color for numbers** ‚Äî draws attention to stats
3. **Generous whitespace** ‚Äî let content breathe
4. **Subtle animations** ‚Äî fade in, not flashy
5. **Consistent card style** ‚Äî rounded corners (16px), subtle background
